About us:

Who are we?

Hello! We are a small online spell blog community run by two sisters, Sabrina and Beatrix.

We were raised to live by the seasons and have incorporated this into our practice for many years now.

Together we created The Witches Society.

What is it?

We believe in collaboration between witches worldwide!

We waned to create an online community for witches all over the world where we can share our spells and knowledge with each other.

Think of it as a collaborative Grimoire!

How do I use it?

Anyone can read the spell blog page, we want spell knowledge to be freely available to all!

To sumbit a new spell post to our blog all you need to do is create a user account and head to the 'Submit a spell' area at the top right of the page.

We can't wait to read all of your amazing submissions!
